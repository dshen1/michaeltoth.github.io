About Michael Toth

Find me online:

Michael Toth is currently working in analytics and living in New York City. Today, Michael is working with BlackRock where he is focused on financial risk analytics.

Michael is passionate about using data and statistics to solve challenging business problems and answer interesting questions. Specicically, Michael is interested in machine learning, data analysis, data visualization, and applied statistics.

Michael graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 2011, where he received a BSc in Economics with concentrations in statistics and finance and a minor in mathematics.

When he's not programming or working, Michael enjoys working out, playing chess, and watching movies. If you'd like to get in contact with Michael, please reach out via Facebook or LinkedIn.
